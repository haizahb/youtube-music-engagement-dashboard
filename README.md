Project Overview:

🎵 YouTube Music & Entertainment Channel Engagement Dashboard (Power BI): 
A multi‑page Power BI dashboard analyzing music and entertainment YouTube channels using aggregated engagement metrics such as views, likes and comments. 
This project highlights top‑performing labels, artists, and entertainment creators, revealing audience interaction patterns and engagement distribution across the platform.

🔍 Project Objectives:
- Analyze engagement patterns across music and entertainment channels
- Identify top channels based on views, likes, and comments
- Compare audience interaction across major labels, artists, and creators
- Visualize engagement distribution using intuitive charts
- Build a clean, professional Power BI dashboard suitable for analysis

⭐ Key Insights
1. Overall Engagement Is Strong but Uneven Across Videos

- A small number of videos drive the majority of total engagement (likes, comments, shares), indicating that content performance is highly concentrated.
- This suggests opportunities to replicate the formats or topics of top‑performing videos.

2. Watch Time and Retention Reveal Clear Viewer Preferences

- Videos with higher retention rates consistently align with longer watch times, showing that viewers stay engaged when content pacing, structure, or topic resonates.
- Lower‑retention videos may need improved intros or more compelling hooks.

3. Subscriber Growth Is Influenced by Specific Content Types

- Certain uploads correlate with noticeable spikes in subscriber gains, suggesting that specific themes or formats attract new audiences more effectively than others.

4. Engagement Rate Highlights High‑Impact Content

- Videos with moderate view counts but high engagement rates indicate strong community interaction.
- These videos are valuable for strengthening audience loyalty even if they don’t go viral.

5. Upload Frequency Impacts Channel Momentum

- Periods with consistent uploads show healthier engagement and subscriber trends, while gaps in publishing correlate with dips in performance.
- Maintaining a steady cadence helps sustain audience interest.

6. Traffic Sources Show Where Viewers Discover Content
- External sources (e.g., search, suggested videos) play a major role in visibility.
- Videos that perform well in YouTube Search tend to have stronger long‑term engagement, highlighting the importance of optimized titles and metadata.
